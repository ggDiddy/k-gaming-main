Is online casino gambling legal in Canada?

+

Online casino gambling is regulated in Ontario through iGaming Ontario. In other provinces, offshore licensed casinos operate in a legal gray zone and are widely accessible. No Canadian player has ever been prosecuted for playing at offshore sites.

Can I use Interac to deposit at online casinos?

+

Yes! Interac is Canada's most popular casino payment method. Both Interac Online and Interac e-Transfer are widely supported. Deposits are instant and withdrawals typically take 1–3 business days. All casinos in our list accept Interac.

Do I need to pay tax on casino winnings in Canada?

+

Generally, casual gambling winnings are not taxable in Canada. The CRA considers gambling a form of entertainment, not income. However, if you gamble professionally or systematically for profit, your winnings may be subject to income tax.

Are bonuses available in Canadian dollars (CAD)?

+

Yes, all casinos listed on this page support Canadian dollars. You won't lose money on currency conversion fees. Bonuses are credited directly in CAD and all transaction limits shown are in Canadian dollars.

What is the minimum age to gamble online in Canada?

+

The minimum gambling age in Canada is 18 in Alberta, Manitoba, and Quebec, and 19 in all other provinces. All casinos on our list enforce age verification and comply with their respective jurisdictions.
